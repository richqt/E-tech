Ateneo De Manila University

Ateneo De Manila University is a private, research, basic and higher education institution founded and run by the Society of Jesus in Quezon City, Philippines. Its main campus is in Loyola Heights but has different campuses in different areas of the Philippines.

Univerisity of the East

University of the East is one of the most prestigious University in the Philippines. It is located in Manila, Philippines and has three campuses around Luzon. It once had the title of the “largest university in Asia” when its enrollees reached more than 65,000 students.

University of Santo Tomas

University of Santo Tomas or UST is Asia's oldest university. In terms of student numbers, it is the world's largest Catholic university on a single campus. Bishop Miguel de Benavides, O.P., the third Archbishop of Manila, spearheaded the establishment of the school.

National Univerisity

The National University, sometimes known as National U, is a private, non-sectarian, coeducational university in Sampaloc, Manila, Philippines. Mariano F. Jhocson Sr., the university's founder, founded the college on August 1, 1900, as Colegio Filipino in Quiapo, Manila

De La Salle Univerisity

De La Salle University positions itself as a leader in molding human resources who serve the church and the nation.It is a Catholic coeducational institution.The University is a hub for higher education training renowned for its academic excellence, prolific and relevant research, and involved community service.

Far Eastern University

Far Eastern University has long been regarded as one of the top universities in the Philippines and aims to be a university of choice in Asia. FEU has had long and rich history in education, culture, arts and sports. The university strives to impart excellent education by providing alternatives to students.

Learn More
University of the Philippines

The University of the Philippines (UP) is the country’s national university. This premier institution of higher learning was established in 1908 and is now a university system composed of eight constituent universities spread throughout 17 campuses all over the archipelago.

Learn More
Adamson Univerisity

Adamson University is a Catholic university in Manila's downtown area. The Adamson School of Industrial Chemistry was created in 1932 by Dr. George Lucas Adamson, a Greek scientist. In 1941, it was designated as a university, and in 1964, the Congregation of the Mission, also known as the Vincentians, took over as the new proprietors and administrators.
